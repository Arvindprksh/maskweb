WHAT WE DO?

Getting through to people through theatre

We are a prodigious team of purpose-driven youngsters who understand that only when people come together does change happens. We raise funds through arts and entertainment, to eradicate the issues in the society by empowering the victims and enlightening the world, through awareness campaigns, about the cause to which they turn a blind eye.

The Mask Team


About Us:

The number of NGOs in India is greater than the number of issues faced by the society. Yet, the change happens very slowly.The Mask is a youth volunteer non-profit organization founded by a group of socially conscious college students with a strong passion to build a society that is devoid of all the social evils faced by the underprivileged today. We realized that an issue cannot be eliminated from the society by only helping the victims through monetary help and services. But by creating awareness about the cause through extensive campaigns that enlighten the masses. This is accomplished by organising grand fundraisers from which every single rupee generated is directly used for the services for the targeted community. We aim to create a platform and bring together all like minded individuals who wish to give back to the society and make a difference. The volunteers in turn get a perspective-changing experience that hardens their sense of social responsibility thus making them leaders of tomorrow. We target different issues, from the ones that are seemingly innocuous to the ones that are blatantly publicly visible. We strive to identify and fight for the causes.We strive to bring together people who are looking for a platform to serve the society. Fundraiser: Our fundraiser is the quintessential yet immortally magical, theatre play. Theatre, considered a dying art, once flourishing as the only form of entertainment, is now suffering at the hands of cinema. Despite actors unequivocally admitting to theatre being their first love, theatre seems to be grossly neglected. Theatre has something that cinema can never offer. A sense of realism; a feeling of spontaneity; an impact of audience being emotionally invested in the actors on stage without any special effects to woo the audience rather winning their hearts by soul-gripping script performed by charismatic actors. Theatre is not a dying art, it's just a struggling art that needs passionate admirers to come together and deliver it to the larger audience for its revival.
